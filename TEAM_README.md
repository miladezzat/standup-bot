## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where do I type `/standup`?**
A: Anywhere in Slack - any channel, any DM, or message the bot directly.

**Q: What if I made a mistake?**
A: Just type `/standup` again and resubmit. It will update your standup for today.

**Q: Do I have to submit on weekends?**
A: No! The bot only sends reminders Mon-Fri, but you can submit anytime if needed.

**Q: What if I'm on leave or sick?**
A: Just don't submit - no worries! Or submit a quick note like "On leave today" if you prefer.

**Q: How do I know my standup was saved?**
A: You'll get a confirmation DM from the bot immediately after submitting.

**Q: Can my manager see my standups?**
A: Yes, all standups are visible to the team and managers via the dashboard.

**Q: What if the bot doesn't respond when I type `/standup`?**
A: Make sure you typed it correctly with the `/` at the start. If still not working, contact your team lead.

**Q: Can I submit after 9:30 AM?**
A: Yes! 9:30 AM is the target, but you can submit anytime during the day.

**Q: What counts as a blocker?**
A: Something that actually stops you from making progress - need approval, waiting for someone, missing access, unclear requirements, etc.

**Q: How long should my standup be?**
A: 3-5 items per section is ideal. Each item should be 1-2 lines.

**Q: Can I use emojis?**
A: Sure! Whatever helps communicate clearly.
